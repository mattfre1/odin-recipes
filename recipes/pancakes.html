Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 5 cups whole wheat flour
  • 3 cups unbleached all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 2 ½ tablespoons baking powder
  • 4 teaspoons baking soda
  • 4 teaspoons salt

Step-by-step

  1. Put all Ingredients into bowl.
  2. Whisk in the bowl.
  3. Let it rest for 30 min.
  4. Heat up the pan.
  5. Fry pancakes in the pan.
  6. Put your favourite cream on it.
